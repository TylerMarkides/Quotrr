Have Questions?

How does Quotrr generate accurate quotes for my business? +

Quotrr uses your uploaded price list to create quotes. Whether a customer types out their request or chooses items manually, the system matches their input to your services and pricing to generate an accurate estimate.

Can I customise the look and feel of my quote form? +

Yes - on our paid plans, you can add your logo and business details. On the Scale plan, you can fully customise colours and remove all Quotrr branding for a completely white-labelled experience.

What happens after a customer submits a quote? +

You’ll get the quote in your dashboard, where you can edit, approve, and send it back to the customer. They’ll receive it by email with an “Accept” button and a payment link.

How do I upload or update my pricing list? +

You can upload your pricing list directly in your dashboard as a PDF or CSV. You can also update your prices any time, and the changes will apply instantly.

What’s the difference between AI and manual quoting? +

With AI quoting, customers describe what they need in their own words and the system figures out the right services. Manual quoting lets them choose from a structured list - both options lead to the same accurate quote.

Can I track and manage quotes from my dashboard? +

Yes - your dashboard shows all quotes in one place. You can edit, resend, track status (like sent, accepted, paid), and even upgrade your plan if you’re reaching your monthly limit, or pay for more individual quotes.
